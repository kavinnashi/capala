Our Approach

Our approach is to be the preferred partners for businesses and investors likewise. Drawing on our extensive network, experience in the market and primary research, we provide global investors with timely, accurate and meaningful insights.

We work with regional & global funds, family offices, and HNIs on identification and assessment of potential investment opportunities.

What we look for !

As part of the Capala philosophy, Capala Partner Investors typically make investments in companies requiring $500K to $5M or less in new capital and target high growth, disruptive tech solutions.

Early & Growth Stage

We like to work with Early Stage tech solutions that challenge the status quo and have the ability to provide exceptional returns on investments. Our Focus on Growth Stage Companies is limited to organizations who have a steady cash flow and are looking for working capital for new initiatives.

Industry

We work with sectors which focus on tech disruptions to maximise on reach and scale. These are some of the things at which we are actively looking: Marketplaces,Agritech, HealthTech , Deep Tech Solutions, , Cyber Security, Robotics and AI.

Geography-- US, Europe, India & Japan

Our Partner Investors invest extensively in companies that are founded in US, Europe, India & Japan. The technology infrastructure in these regions and the entrepreneurial culture provide strong fundamentals to deliver maximum ROI.

Global Platform

We look for companies where our human capital, our global mentor network and our expertise in bridging the North American and South East Asian markets, can be even more valuable than the financial capital we facilitate. We tend to maximise on our expertise and in the process support startups in accelerating the growth.

Deal Terms

We invest between $500K to $5M.
